WHO WE ARE

We are Grade 12 STEM students from Bulacan State University Laboratory High School. We have taken advantage of the environment through the years and it has to stop now. We wanted to raise awareness regarding the consequences of our actions that destroy the environment. Specifically, bodies of water that are home to aquatic plants have also been affected by our selfish actions. We have the highest capability to maintain the beauty of our own surroundings. By doing simple things, we can make a bigger change. Let us all be part of the solution. Make a change because every drop counts.
