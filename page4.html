How did they come up with this idea?

Written by: Angelica Dela Cruz, Charisarae Dela Cruz, Jieline Rianne Sonza


Two of the developers of this website, Charisarae Dela Cruz and
Jieline Rianne Sonza, suggested that the subject of this website
will be the creek located at Ma. Lourdes Subdivision, Tabang,
Plaridel, Bulacan. The two students happen to pass by this creek on
a daily basis on their way to school. It caught their attention
because it is quite noticeable as the creek smells slightly unpleasant
due to the galore amount of trash that has been accumulated.
It was evident that the unpleasant state of the creek consequently
affects the residents living near that area especially that this
creek is located in a subdivision. Thus, the students wanted to
at least make an action about this issue by featuring it on this
website as their topic of interest.

They conducted an interview among the residents who live in the
said subdivision that are affected by the polluted creek. They
were asked to rate the current state of the creek. They were also asked
to describe how this issue has been negatively affecting their
way of living. Most of them stated how poor the sanitation is and
that it has been evidently affecting them in a way that it can cause
diseases. 

They wanted to raise awareness about the consequences of our actions
that can affect our nature. Additionally, the adverse effects are not
limited only to our nature for it also impacts us in a way that it affects our
health and way of living.
